About Gunni Traders

Founded by Shiv Chanpuria in June 2016, Gunni Traders has been a trusted provider of high-quality construction materials and hardware in Katni, Madhya Pradesh. Since its inception, the company has been committed to delivering excellence to contractors, builders, and homeowners across the region.

We take pride in offering top-grade products sourced from trusted manufacturers, ensuring that our customers receive materials that meet the highest industry standards. Our experienced and knowledgeable staff is always available to help you find the right solutions tailored to your project needs.

At Gunni Traders, we focus on building strong, lasting relationships with our customers through exceptional service, competitive pricing, and dependable delivery. Whether you're a professional contractor or a passionate DIY enthusiast, we have everything you need to successfully bring your construction projects to life.
